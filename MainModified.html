Herbal Neem

The neem plant is a versatile, evergreen tree known for its medicinal properties, drought resistance, and ability to thrive in harsh climates.

Herbal Tulsi

The Tulsi plant, also known as holy basil, is a scared, aromatic herb revered for its medicinal, spiritual, and purifying properties.

Sanchezia

Sanchezia is a tropical evergreen shrub known for its striking, glossy green leaves with bold yellow veins and its tubular, bright yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators.
